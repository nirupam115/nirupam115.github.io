Hello! Thanks for dropping by.
I am a postdoc in the Distributed Computing Laboratory (DCL), which is directed by Prof. Rachid Guerraoui and is part of the School of Computer Science at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne (a.k.a. EPFL). In the past, I have also had the pleasure of working with Prof. Nitin Vaidya and Prof. Nikhil Chopra (my PhD advisor at University of Maryland). For my past affiliations and education, please refer to my CV (link to which is provided in the blue box). My general research interest is distributed machine learning and optimization. Currently, I focus primarily on the challenges of robustness to adversarial data and computing units in distributed ML. List of all my publications can be found on my DBLP page. I have co-authored the book Robust Machine Learning: Distributed Methods for Safe AI, which presents a pedagogical introduction to the topic of Robust ML, specifically in distributed frameworks such as Federated Learning.
