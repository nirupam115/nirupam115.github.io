निरुपम गुप्ता / Nirupam Gupta

I am an (TT) Assistant Professor in the ML Section of the Department of Computer Science at University of Copenhagen (DIKU). My contact information can be found on my CV.

Research. I work on Distributed Machine Learning (a.k.a. Federated Learning), focusing on the challenges of robustness, privacy and fairness. Updated list of my publications can be found on my DBLP page. To learn about the basics of robustness in the context of distributed learning, you are welcome to check out my book Robust Machine Learning: Distributed Methods for Safe AI.

Teaching. I am co-teaching Privacy in Machine Learning (PriMaL) and Machine Learning B (MLB) at DIKU, in the academic year 2024-2025. Both these courses are offered in a hybrid format, and support fully remote participation. For more details, check out this website for ML courses at DIKU.

Open Positions!! I am looking for PhD students who would be interested in the topics of robustness, privacy and fairness in machine learning, especially in the context of modern language models. To know more on these topics and their critical role in safe and responsible AI, take a quick look at my aforementioned book and this book on LLMs on Cybersecurity.
See this joint call for PhD fellowships to apply for the position, and to learn more about other open PhD positions at DIKU. Deadline: January 15, 2025.
